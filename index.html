A Raven

You may not leave
the battlefield grim
the light in your eyes dim
you must stay

or so they say.

Do not think it fine
to let wings fly dripping
to the sun above the world
you must stay

or so they say.

Claim the right
to slip into endless night
and taste the fire
of endless light

that's what they say.

But if I decide to fly
breathless and tired
and speak to a raven
would he take me?

or would I crumple to the ground?
